Who am I?

My name is Ekim Tektas, and I am currently working towards getting my second Master's in English Literature. I am currently an English teacher at a high school in Fairfax County Public Schools. This year I worked with freshmen (Class of 2029) and sophomores (Class of 2028). It was a pleasure working with them this since I was able to see them grow as individuals!

As an educator, at the beginning of the year, we are tasked with creating a goal we would like students to achieve prior to the end of the year. My goal was for students to improve on their written communication since there is a current stagnation for students to write because of consistent use of spellcheck and not having the stamina for it.
